Why Compress Your Images?

Image optimization is crucial for website performance, user experience, and SEO. Large images slow down your site, increase bounce rates, and can negatively impact your search engine rankings.

Key Benefits

  • Faster loading times: Optimized images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Better SEO: Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Reduced bandwidth: Save on hosting costs and data usage
  • Improved conversions: Faster sites lead to higher conversion rates

How It Works

  1. Upload your images (drag & drop or click to browse)
  2. Adjust compression settings to your preference
  3. Click "Compress Images" and wait a few seconds
  4. Download your optimized files with a single click

Best Practices

  • For photos: Use JPEG or WebP format with 70-80% quality
  • For graphics with transparency: Use PNG or WebP
  • Resize images to their display dimensions before uploading
  • Test different quality levels to find the right balance
